Reservation Spreadsheet

When I got a job as a reservationist back in 2022 I didn’t realize it would re-spark my interest in spreadsheets and lead to learning some incredible functions to build complex things. This is a self-updating floor chart, designed to show reservations for specific tables on the right as the reservations are entered to the left. There’s also a series of graphs on the bottom right showing various metrics for the night, such as the amount of guests entering at various times and the total number of guests in the restaurant for the night. The spreadsheet’s format was built with printing in mind, so the kitchen, hosts, and managers had easy visualization to prepare for the night. To this day, this is one of my proudest projects.
